The founders

Meet the owners

The passionate women behind EFFOI Restaurant & Bar

Co-Founder
Marye Effoi
Co-Founder & Executive Chef

With over 20 years of culinary experience, Marye brings the authentic flavours of Ethiopia to every dish. Trained by her grandmother in the traditional cooking methods of Addis Ababa, she ensures every recipe maintains its cultural integrity while delighting modern palates.

"Cooking is my love language. Every dish tells a story of our heritage."
Co-Founder
Nigist Effoi
Co-Founder & Hospitality Director

Nigist's journey in hospitality began in her family's traditional coffee ceremony in Ethiopia. Her warm smile and genuine care for guests create the unforgettable atmosphere that EFFOI is known for.

"Hospitality is not a service — it's a tradition passed through generations."

"Together, we built EFFOI to share the rich culinary heritage of Ethiopia with our community. Every dish, every smile, every moment is a reflection of our love for our culture and our guests."

M.E.  &  N.E.
