Meet The Owners

The passionate women behind EFFOI Restaurant & Bar

ME
Co-Founder

Marye Effoi

Co-Founder & Executive Chef

With over 20 years of culinary experience, Marye brings the authentic flavors of Ethiopia to every dish. Trained by her grandmother in the traditional cooking methods of Addis Ababa, she ensures that every recipe maintains its cultural integrity while delighting modern palates.

"Cooking is my love language. Every dish tells a story of our heritage."
NE
Co-Founder

Nigist Effoi

Co-Founder & Hospitality Director

Nigist's journey in hospitality began in her family's traditional coffee ceremony in Ethiopia. Her warm smile and genuine care for guests create the unforgettable atmosphere that EFFOI is known for. She ensures that every visitor feels like family from the moment they walk through the door.

"Hospitality is not a service, it's a tradition passed down through generations."

Together, we built EFFOI to share the rich culinary heritage of Ethiopia with our community. Every dish, every smile, every moment is a reflection of our love for our culture and our guests.

M.E. & N.E.

The EFFOI Family

The dedicated team behind your unforgettable dining experience

One Team, One Family

At EFFOI, our team is the heartbeat of everything we do. From our culinary artists who craft each dish with precision, to our service staff who greet you with genuine smiles – every member of our family is dedicated to creating unforgettable moments for our guests.

What makes us special isn't just our skills, but our spirit. We celebrate together, support each other, and grow as one. This family atmosphere radiates from our kitchen to every table, ensuring that when you dine with us, you're truly dining with family.

The EFFOI Team
